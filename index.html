About Me

My passion for software development extends beyond testing, as I also thoroughly enjoy building websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. This combination of skills allows me to approach software testing with a developer's mindset, ensuring the highest level of quality and efficiency in every project I undertake. I am constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to enhance my skills and contribute to innovative and impactful software solutions.
